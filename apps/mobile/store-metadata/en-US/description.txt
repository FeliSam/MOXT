MOXT — the services platform connecting Africa and Russia.

Send and track transfers, publish or book traveler parcels, buy and sell on the marketplace, find jobs or trusted businesses — built for the Afro-Russian diaspora (Benin / West Africa ↔ Russia).

FEATURES
• Transfers and tracking across communities
• Traveler parcels with in-app messaging
• Marketplace (listings, favorites, search)
• Jobs and businesses
• Identity verification and trust score
• Push notifications (messages, transfers, subscriptions)
• UI in FR / RU / EN / PT

SECURITY
• Protected account, optional KYC verification
• No SMS read access and no required GPS location
• Privacy policy: https://moxtapp.ru/legal/privacy
